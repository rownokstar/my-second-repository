Saint Martin tour with my special one

Author : Traveller Dihan Chowdhury

St. Martin's Island is a popular tourist destination. During the tourist season, 5 yachts and cruise ships available in route linking the mainland and island of Bangladesh every day. To appreciate its enchanting beauty, one night is never enough. It is better to have two nights. But it's for sure, if you traveled here once in your lifetime, it will lure you again. On the first day, You can enjoy the Chera Dwip. And another day of the trip can be allocated to St. Martin. Every day tourists return in the afternoon. So the experience of visiting the island in the afternoon is quite different. And if you happen to visit the island on a full moon night, prepare to face the fascination of staying more.

Sundorbon a Bagh mama halim khay

Author : Traveller Dihan Chowdhury

Introduce of Natural beauty Sundarban in Bangladesh. The Sundarbans has been considered as the largest mangrove forest in the world consisting of the cluster of the island having diverse and rich natural resource and intersected by a network of rivers and channels. The Sundarbans is a very unique habitual and ecosystem that has been recognized globally for its importance in terms of Biodiversity and resources. The Sundarbans support a wide range of important flora and fauna, both in quantity and diversity, which are endangered, both domestically and worldwide. Presence of the Royal Bengal Tiger, estuarine crocodile, a variety of dolphins, reptiles and a certain number of birds and highly endangered species has created the lot of importance to eco-tourism in Sundarbans.
